About Me

John Dave Corpuz Salomon also known as “Jeidi” is an aspiring independent musician from the Philippines. He started making songs at the age of 17, and with the help of using the Looper Packs in an app to create his tracks, he released his first single “Slowly” in 2022 which was all produced, mixed, and recorded using his phone. Now with his latest EP “sincerely happy,” he shares his experience and struggles with depression and procrastination which he turned into 2 tracks that are in this release.

Song Releases

sincerely happy

'sincerely happy' is an EP of mine and I wrote the following tracks to share my experience and struggles with depression and procrastination, especially when it comes to dealing with schoolworks.

why can't we?

'why can't we?' is the second track of my EP. I wrote this song for people to relate of how hard it is to deal with procrastination, especially during schooldays or dealing with schoolworks and also to remind them that things will eventually be fine as long as we try to look for a solution.

shallow

My bestfriend wrote this song from some old beat that I gave him. I can't really relate to the lyrics of this song, this was just some random song me and my bestfriend created when we got united again. This song talks about someone who still can't get over with their ex.

heartache

'heartache' is a song I wrote for my first love which I fumbled so hard. This song is for those people who messed up their shot but already accepted the fact that they lost.

slowly

'slowly' is a song I wrote for my bestfriend and my band when we seperated ways. This song is for those people who misses their best bud.

Dreamelody

'Dreamelody' is a song me and my friends made to serve as a reminder to us that we should never give up on our dreams and that we should really fight for who we really want to be in our lives, as well as to make tons of memories with each other before one of us leaves or all of us get separated. This song was just supposed to be a school project but it got real.
